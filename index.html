About

Humidity_tempreture IOT mentoring system

Climate change is one of the biggest global problems facing the world especially Egypt these days. In our continuous quest to solve the problems of Egypt, “reduce and adapt to climate change” has been worked on to solve it this semester. That happened using IoT technology and wireless communication to measure two indicators and show their effect on the climate to be able to provide a solution with high accuracy. Good measuring enabled us to know the size of the problem and know all its aspects. The design requirements chosen for our solution are (high accuracy and a large dynamic range). The solution chosen is an IOT mentoring system to detect the relative humidity and temperature using ESP8266 DHT11/DHT22 Temperature and Humidity Web Server with Arduino IDE. Then a simulation was made for our solution to simulate the natural air using 3 bulbs as a source of the temperature to be able to control the temperature. Then three tests were done on the prototype and in every trial, the sensor detected the temperature and humidity every ten seconds. Then a proposal was given to avoid the negative impacts of climate change in Egypt.
